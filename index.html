About Us

3 Stacks, a burger joint situated in Al Muwaihat 3 of Ajman, serves up delectable smash burgers that start at an economical 10 dirhams. Using premium ingredients, they aim to provide the ultimate burger experience. Patrons can expect exceptional service, cementing 3Stacks’ commitment to culinary excellence and dedication to leaving a lasting impression on Ajman's food scene.
